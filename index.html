The reception will start at 5pm. We have exclusive use of the upper restaurant until 12 midnight. There will be a bar serving drinks. We can also use the main bar downstairs. A buffet will be provided until 7pm.

Due to the limited capacity of the venue, only those people named on the invites will be able to attend. We politely ask that you do not bring any plus ones.
