The Ceremony

This will take place at Stamford Registry Office, and will begin at 4pm. Please ensure that you arrive at least 15 minutes before this to take your seat.

We politely request that no confetti, rice, or similar is thrown after the ceremony.  ⇓

The Reception

After the ceremony we will make our way to All Saints Bar for approximately 5pm. We have exclusive use of the upper room until 12 midnight. There is a bar downstairs serving cocktails, bottled beers, wine, and spirits. They accept both cash and card. A small buffet will be provided.

All Saints is a bar. Therefore, no one under the age of 18 will be allowed on the premises. We cannot make exceptions to this as it is the policy of the venue.

Due to the limited capacity of All Saints, only those people named on the invites will be able to attend. We politely ask that you do not bring any plus ones.
